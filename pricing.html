Our promise

At Cherry Street Labs we work with fixed pricing because we ship fast and predictably. Every project starts with a signed scope document that lists exact features, platforms, integrations, and success metrics.

Our Scope Protection Clause

Included in every contract.

Pricing is fixed for the exact scope documented in our signed agreement. Timeline assumes your feedback within 48 hours on all reviews. Any scope changes, new requirements, or delays outside our control (e.g., waiting on your API keys, third-party approvals, or payment methods) will be handled via a documented change order or switch to our $7,000/month retainer (billed in advance, pro-rated).
